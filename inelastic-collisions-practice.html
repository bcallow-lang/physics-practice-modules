Perfectly Inelastic Collisions

Core Equation (Conservation of Momentum):

m1v1i + m2v2i = (m1 + m2)vf

In a perfectly inelastic collision, two objects collide and stick together, moving with a single, shared final velocity (vf).

m1, m2 = mass of objects (kg)
v1i, v2i = initial velocities (m/s)
vf = final velocity (m/s)
