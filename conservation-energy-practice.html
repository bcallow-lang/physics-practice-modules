Conservation of Energy Practice

Core Equation:

KE1 + PEg1 = KE2 + PEg2

Component Equations:

KE = 1/2 * m * v² PEg = m * g * h
KE = Kinetic Energy (J)
PEg = Gravitational Potential Energy (J)
m = mass (kg)
v = velocity (m/s)
h = height (m)
g ≈ 9.8 m/s²
