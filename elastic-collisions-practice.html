Elastic Collisions Practice

Core Equation (Conservation of Momentum):

m1v1i + m2v2i = m1v1f + m2v2f

In an elastic collision, two objects collide and bounce off each other. Momentum is conserved.

m1, m2 = mass of objects (kg)
v1i, v2i = initial velocities (m/s)
v1f, v2f = final velocities (m/s)
