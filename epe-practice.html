Elastic Potential Energy Practice

Core Equation:

EPE = 1/2 * k * x²

Related Equation (Displacement from Equilibrium):

x = xf - xi
EPE = Elastic Potential Energy (Joules, J)
k = spring constant (units: N/m)
x = displacement from equilibrium (units: m)
xi, xf = initial/final position (units: m)
