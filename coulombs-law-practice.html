Coulomb's Law Practice

Core Equation:

Fe = k
|q1q2| r²

Scientific Notation Tip:

To type 9.0 × 109, you can type 9.0e9 into the answer box.

Fe = Electric Force (Newtons, N)
k = Coulomb's Constant (≈ 9.0 × 109 N·m²/C²)
q1, q2 = Charges (Coulombs, C)
r = Distance between charges (meters, m)
