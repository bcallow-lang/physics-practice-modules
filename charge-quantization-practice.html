Charge Quantization Practice

Core Equation:

q = n * e

Scientific Notation Tip:

To type 1.6 × 10-19, you can type 1.6e-19 into the answer box.

q = Net Charge (Coulombs, C)
n = Number of elementary charges (integer)
e = Elementary Charge constant
e ≈ 1.60 × 10-19 C
