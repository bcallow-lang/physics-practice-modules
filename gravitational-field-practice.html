Core Equation:

g = G

Scientific Notation Tip:

To type 6.67 × 10-11, you can type 6.67e-11 into the answer box.

g = Gravitational Field Strength (N/kg or m/s²)
G = Gravitational Constant (≈ 6.67 × 10-11)
m = Mass of source object (kg)
r = Distance from center (m)
