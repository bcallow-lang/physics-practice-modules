Unbalanced Forces Practice (Newton's 2nd Law)

Core Equation:

Fnet = m * a

When forces are unbalanced (Fnet is not zero), an object will accelerate. Net Force is the sum of all forces.

Vertical Acceleration

Fnet, y = FTension - Fg = m*a

Horizontal Acceleration

Fnet, x = Fapplied - Ffriction = m*a
Fnet = Net Force (N)
m = mass (kg)
a = acceleration (m/s²)
Fg = m * g
g ≈ 9.8 m/s²
