Electric Field Strength

Core Equation:

E = k
Q r²

Scientific Notation Tip:

To type 9.0 × 109, you can type 9.0e9 into the answer box.

E = Electric Field Strength (N/C)
k = Coulomb's Constant (≈ 9.0 × 109)
Q = Source Charge (C)
r = Distance from charge (m)
