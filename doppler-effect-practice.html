Doppler Effect Practice

General Equation:

fobs = fs
v ± vobs v ∓ vs

Sign Convention Rules:

Top Signs (+ top, - bottom)

Use when motion is TOWARDS

Observer towards source (+)
Source towards observer (-)

Bottom Signs (- top, + bottom)

Use when motion is AWAY

Observer away from source (-)
Source away from observer (+)

fobs = Observed Frequency (Hz)
fs = Source Frequency (Hz)
v = Speed of Sound (m/s)
vobs = Observer Velocity (m/s)
vs = Source Velocity (m/s)
