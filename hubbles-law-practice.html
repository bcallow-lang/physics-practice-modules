Hubble's Law Practice

Core Equation:

v = H0 * d

Unit Note:

In cosmology, we typically use km/s for velocity and Mpc (Megaparsecs) for distance.

v = Recessional Velocity (km/s)
H0 = Hubble's Constant (≈ 70 km/s/Mpc)
d = Distance (Mpc)
